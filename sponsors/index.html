PARTNERS

SAGE Canada

SAGE (Students for the Advancement of Global Entrepreneurship) is an international network that links high school students with opportunities to make the world a better place by creating and implementing innovative projects. The primary goal of SAGE is to advance youth entrepreneurship and make a difference in the world.

Ryerson DMZ

The DMZ is the #1 university incubator in North America*. It is a resource-centre for a large number of local Canadian start-ups, including many not based at Ryerson University. It is home to entrepreneurs from around the world. Members of the DMZ often develop new technology and accelerate product launches.
