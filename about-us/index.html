ABOUT US

Our Story

In 2015, motivated by a number of high-profile and personal drunk-driving cases, sixteen high-school students came together to form SpitStrips. Today, it has grown from a group of passionate youth into a fully-fledged startup, and it continues to grow and reach new audiences.

Worried by the increasing number of high-profile incidents involving alcohol abuse, we created the SpitStrip. It is a small, paper-based slip that changes colour based on alcohol content in saliva, and can be read using a provided colour-scale.

The current team includes talented managers, public relations officers, specialists in business, biochemistry and programming, and more.
